# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** May 5, 2026

## Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to the Simply Scan app (the "App"), developed and published as a free application. This document is intended to inform users about our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

## No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or usage data of any kind. Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- **No usage data** is tracked or transmitted
- **No analytics** tools or third-party SDKs are integrated into the App
- **No advertising networks** are used
- **No cookies** or similar tracking technologies are employed
- **No account registration** is required

All data generated by the App (such as counter values) is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it.

## Third-Party Services

This App does not integrate any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Children's Privacy

This App does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. It is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

## Changes to This Policy

If our privacy practices change in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly. Any changes will be reflected by a new "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
